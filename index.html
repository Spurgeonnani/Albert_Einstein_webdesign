Albert Einstein

Biography

Albert Einstein was born in Germany on March 14, 1879. He started his education in Munich but later moved to Switzerland, where he continued his studies. In 1896, he joined the Swiss Federal Polytechnic School in Zurich to study physics and mathematics. After earning his diploma in 1901 and gaining Swiss citizenship, he worked as a technical assistant at the Swiss Patent Office. In 1905, he completed his doctorate.
